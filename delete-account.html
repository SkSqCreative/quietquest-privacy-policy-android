Delete Your QuietQuest Account

⚠️ Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your QuietQuest account in two ways:

Option 1: Delete from the App

1 Open the QuietQuest app and log in to your account
2 Navigate to Settings (gear icon)
3 Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account"
4 Confirm your decision when prompted

Option 2: Email Request

1 Send an email to info@skillfulsquirrel.com
2 Include your account email address in the request
3 We will process your request within 48 hours

What Data is Deleted?

Data Type Action Timeline
Account Information Permanently deleted Immediately
Email Address Permanently deleted Immediately
Username Permanently deleted Immediately
Quiet Time Sessions Permanently deleted Immediately
Progress & Statistics Permanently deleted Immediately
Badges & Achievements Permanently deleted Immediately
App Settings & Preferences Permanently deleted Immediately
Purchase History Anonymized and retained For tax compliance (7 years)

Important Notes

  • Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered
  • You will lose access to all your quiet time history and progress
  • Any active subscriptions will be cancelled (Note: QuietQuest currently has no subscriptions)
  • Purchase records are anonymized and kept for legal compliance only
  • It may take up to 30 days for your data to be completely removed from all backup systems

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • All personal data is immediately deleted from active systems
  • Backup systems are purged within 30 days
  • Anonymized purchase records are retained for 7 years for tax compliance
  • No personal information remains that can identify you
