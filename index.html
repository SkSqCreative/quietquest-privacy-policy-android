QuietQuest Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2025

Introduction

QuietQuest ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Account information (email address, username)
  • Quiet time session data (duration, timestamps)
  • App preferences and settings
  • In-app purchase information (processed by Google Play)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Track your quiet time progress and achievements
  • Send you notifications (if enabled)
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve our app and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a trusted backend service provider. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your quiet time data is private and only accessible to you.

We may share information with:

  • Service providers (Supabase for data storage, Sentry for error tracking)
  • Payment processors (Google Play for in-app purchases)
  • Law enforcement when required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your quiet time history
  • Opt-out of notifications

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication
  • Google Play Services: For in-app purchases
  • Sentry: For error tracking and app stability

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
